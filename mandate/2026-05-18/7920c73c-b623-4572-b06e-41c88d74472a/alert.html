Deal summary
Granite Construction Incorporated (NYSE: GVA, market cap $6.1bn), one of the largest diversified, vertically integrated civil contractors and construction materials producers in the U.S., is bringing a $600 million 8-year Senior Notes offering (NC-3) to market via a 144A/Reg S for life format. The deal is led by a broad syndicate of six joint bookrunners (BofA, Barclays, BMO, WF, PNC, JPM) and five co-managers, with proceeds intended primarily to refinance the company's 2028 Convertible Notes and repay revolving credit facility borrowings. Expected pricing is May 19, 2025, following an investor call held May 18, 2025 at 10:30 AM ET.
