Deal summary
Mobility Global Inc. (MBGL), a newly formed automotive data and insights company being spun off from S&P Global, is arranging a series of fixed income investor calls on May 18, 2026, ahead of a potential 144A/Reg S senior unsecured notes offering with registration rights. The deal is expected to consist of 3-, 5-, and 10-year tranches, subject to market conditions, with anticipated ratings of Baa3 (stable) at Moody's and BBB (stable) at Fitch, placing it firmly in investment grade territory. Goldman Sachs & Co. LLC, Morgan Stanley, and BofA Securities have been mandated as joint bookrunners, with Morgan Stanley coordinating logistics for the investor call series.
