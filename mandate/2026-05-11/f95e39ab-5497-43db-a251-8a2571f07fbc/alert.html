Deal summary
Movida Participações S.A. (Ba3/BB-), the second largest car rental and fleet management company in Brazil, has mandated Morgan Stanley, J.P. Morgan, Bradesco BBI, BNP Paribas, Santander, and UBS Investment Bank as Global Coordinators, alongside Itaú BBA, MUFG, Natixis, and XP Investments as Joint Bookrunners, to arrange investor calls beginning May 11, 2026, ahead of a potential Rule 144A/Reg S USD-denominated 7-year senior unsecured bond offering via its subsidiary Movida Europe S.A. Concurrently, the company has announced a tender offer for any and all of its outstanding 7.850% senior unsecured notes due 2029, suggesting the new issuance may be used in part to refinance existing debt. The deal is at the Mandate stage with no pricing terms disclosed, subject to market conditions, with settlement expected on a T+5 basis.
