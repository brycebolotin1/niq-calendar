Deal summary
APA Infrastructure Limited (APAAU), guaranteed by APA Group Limited and rated Baa2/BBB (Stable) by Moody's/S&P, has mandated ANZ, Barclays/Barrenjoey, CBA, NAB, and Westpac as Joint Lead Managers for a potential AUD-denominated benchmark-sized senior unsecured and/or subordinated notes offering across multiple tenors (7.5-year, 10-year, 30NC7.5, and/or 30NC10). The deal is at the Mandate stage, with investor meetings scheduled across Melbourne, Sydney, Singapore, and Hong Kong commencing Monday 13 April 2026, and a group investor call on the same date at 11:00am AEST. Senior notes are expected to be rated Baa2/BBB while subordinated notes are expected to be rated Baa3 by Moody's, with Barrenjoey acting as Structuring Adviser for the potential subordinated notes.
