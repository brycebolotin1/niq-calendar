Deal summary
Oceânica Engenharia e Consultoria S.A., a Brazilian oil and gas subsea services company, has mandated seven joint bookrunners — Goldman Sachs, Itaú BBA, BTG Pactual, Santander, UBS Investment Bank, Bradesco BBI, and BCP Securities — to arrange fixed income investor calls ahead of a potential USD-denominated benchmark-sized Rule 144A/Reg S senior secured 5-year bond offering. The deal is rated B3/B- (Moody's/Fitch), firmly in high-yield territory, and proceeds are intended to fund a tender offer for existing USD 2029 notes and general corporate purposes. No pricing terms have been disclosed at this mandate stage, and the offering remains subject to market conditions.
