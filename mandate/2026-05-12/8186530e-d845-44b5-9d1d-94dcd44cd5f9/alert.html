Deal summary
Medline Borrower, LP & Medline Co-Issuer, Inc. has mandated J.P. Morgan, BofA Securities, and Goldman Sachs & Co. LLC to arrange fixed income investor calls on May 12, 2026, ahead of a potential USD-denominated 144A/Reg S senior secured offering subject to market conditions. The deal carries split ratings of Ba1 (Stable) / BBB- (Stable) / BBB (Stable) by Moody's, S&P, and Fitch respectively, placing it in split-rated territory with two investment grade agency ratings. No size, tenor, or pricing terms have been disclosed at this mandate stage. J.P. Morgan will be coordinating logistics.
